    Designed & Built by Prasad Rawas

    FreeVPN is an open-source VPN app built for Android that helps you browse the internet privately and securely. Connect to 100+ servers across the globe with a single tap. No account creation, no signup, and no personal data collection. Powered by OpenVPN, one of the most trusted and widely used VPN protocols, FreeVPN encrypts your internet traffic and protects your online activity from prying eyes. Whether you're on public Wi-Fi at a cafe, traveling abroad, or simply want more privacy while browsing, FreeVPN has you covered. # KEY FEATURES 1. One-Tap Connect: Get connected to a VPN server instantly. FreeVPN automatically selects the most reliable and fastest server for you using smart auto-connect. You can also choose a server manually from the full server list. 2. 100+ Global Servers: Access a wide network of VPN servers located in countries around the world, including Japan, South Korea, the United States, and many more. Servers are sourced from the VPN Gate Academic Project, a public relay network operated by the University of Tsukuba, Japan. 3. Real-Time Connection Stats: Monitor your VPN connection in real time with live download and upload speed indicators, total data transferred, connection duration, and a connection quality badge (Good, Fair, or Poor) based on ping and throughput. 4. Server Filtering & Search: Browse servers by country using filter chips, search by name or hostname, view your recently connected servers, or pick from your saved favorites. Servers are sorted by reliability, speed, and ping so you always see the best options first. 5. Data Usage Tracking: Keep track of how much data you use through the VPN. View daily, weekly, and monthly usage breakdowns directly within the app. Your usage stats are stored locally on your device. 6. Privacy Focused: FreeVPN does not require an account, email address, or phone number. There are no analytics tracking your browsing activity. Your connection data stays on your device. The entire source code is publicly available for anyone to inspect and verify. 7. Smart Connection Handling: If a server goes offline during your session, FreeVPN detects it automatically and notifies you so you can switch to another server. During auto-connect, the app silently skips unresponsive servers and moves to the next available one. 8. Favorites & Recent Servers: Save your preferred servers to favorites for quick access. The app also remembers your last 5 connected servers so you can reconnect easily. 9. Background Stability: FreeVPN is designed to maintain a stable connection even when running in the background. The app provides guidance for disabling battery optimization to prevent the system from interrupting your VPN session. 10. Lightweight & Clean Interface: A minimal, dark-themed interface that focuses on what matters: getting you connected quickly. No clutter, no pop-ups, no unnecessary screens. # HOW IT WORKS FreeVPN connects to servers from the VPN Gate network using the OpenVPN protocol. Your internet traffic is routed through an encrypted tunnel to the VPN server, which then forwards your requests to the internet. This masks your IP address and encrypts your data in transit. The app fetches the latest server list periodically and applies quality filters to ensure you only see servers that are online, responsive, and fast enough for a good experience. # OPEN SOURCE FreeVPN is fully open source. The complete source code is available for review, contribution, and verification. Transparency is a core principle. You can see exactly how the app works and confirm that your data is handled responsibly. # PERMISSIONS 1. VPN Service: Required to establish VPN connections 2. Internet: Required to fetch server lists and connect to VPN servers 3. Notifications: Used to show VPN connection status (Android 13+)
